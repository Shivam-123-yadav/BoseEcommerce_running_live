Becoming better

We research and test because we value the pursuit of learning, not just because we want to create amazing produts. We also believe that employees should alwasy have opportunities to grow. And because we celebrate individual passions and interests, growth can occur in any direction that excites and inspires you.

Being together

Our people make Bose unique. Across the company, we encourage open communication and active engagement among employees through groups like Bose Young professionals and Women at Bose, while participation in musical grops and community service groups bring us together for a common cause.

Believing in goodness

It's important for us to be a good business and a good neighbor in the places where we live and work. Our charitable activities aren't limited to corporate partnerships either. We support localized giving, paid employee volunteering, contribution matching and an extensive military program.

What We Do

We are your premier destination for all things Bose. Specializing in both sales and repair services, we offer a comprehensive range of solutions to meet your audio needs.

Sales: Discover the latest innovations from Bose in our extensive collection of audio products. Whether you're looking for wireless speakers, noise-canceling headphones, or home theater systems, we have the perfect solution to elevate your listening experience. Our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you through our selection, ensuring you find the perfect Bose product to suit your preferences and lifestyle.

Repair: Has your Bose product seen better days? Don't worry; our expert technicians are here to breathe new life into your beloved equipment. From troubleshooting and diagnostics to component replacement and software updates, we have the skills and experience to handle a wide range of repairs. Rest assured that your Bose device is in capable hands, and we'll work tirelessly to get it back to its optimal performance.

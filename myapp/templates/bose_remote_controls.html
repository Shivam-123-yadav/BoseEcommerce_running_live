FAQs

Q1. How do I pair a new Bose remote control with my system?

Most Bose remotes can be paired by following the included setup instructions. For advanced pairing, refer to your system's user manual or reach out to our support team for guidance.

Q2. Is every Bose soundbar remote compatible across different models?

No, remotes are model-specific. For example, the remote for Bose Soundbar 700 may not work with the 300. Always check compatibility on the product page or contact us to confirm.

Q3. Can I use a universal remote instead of an original Bose remote control?

While some universal remotes offer limited functionality, they often miss key features. For full compatibility and functionality, we recommend genuine Bose remote controls.

Q4. What should I do if my Bose remote control isn’t responding?

Check the battery, reset your remote, and ensure no obstructions block the signal. If it still doesn’t work, you may need a replacement. Contact our team for diagnosis.

Q5. Do you offer warranty or return options for remotes?

Yes, we offer warranty support on most products and allow returns in case of defective items. Terms are listed on each product page.

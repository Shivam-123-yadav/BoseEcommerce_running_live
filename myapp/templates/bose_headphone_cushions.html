Caring for Your Bose Headphone Cushions

Once you’ve installed your new Bose headphone cushions, here’s how to keep them in top condition:

  • Avoid moisture: While some sweat is natural during usage, store your headphones in a dry, ventilated area.
  • Clean regularly: Wipe the cushions with a soft cloth to remove dirt and oils.
  • Use a carry case: A padded case can protect both your headphones and cushions from damage when not in use.

Doing so can significantly extend the lifespan of your headphones and ensure every session sounds just as incredible as the first.

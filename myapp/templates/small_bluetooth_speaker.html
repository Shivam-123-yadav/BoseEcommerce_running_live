Maintenance Tips for Longevity of Your Small Bluetooth Speaker

Want your small Bluetooth speaker to last for years? Here are some quick tips directly from our service engineers:

  • Keep it dry: Even water-resistant speakers are vulnerable to internal moisture.
  • Avoid full discharges: Recharge before the battery is completely drained.
  • Update firmware regularly: Especially on smart speakers.
  • Use original chargers: Generic chargers can harm the battery.
  • Clean ports and mesh covers regularly to maintain clear sound.

A small Bluetooth speaker typically lasts 3–5 years depending on usage and maintenance. Regular care and software updates can extend its life.

Yes, many Bose speakers are compatible with Bluetooth speakers with wireless mic systems, ideal for virtual meetings or presentations.

Q3. How do I know if my speaker needs servicing or a battery replacement?

If your speaker isn’t holding charge, has distorted audio, or frequently disconnects, bring it to our Bose Service Centre for a free diagnostic.

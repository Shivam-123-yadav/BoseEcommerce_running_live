How to Choose the Best Bluetooth Speaker

Finding the best Bluetooth speaker goes beyond just brand names. Here’s what you should consider:

  • Sound Quality: Look for frequency response clarity, solid bass, and volume without distortion.
  • Battery Life: Portability depends on long-lasting power. Aim for 12–30 hours depending on your usage.
  • Bluetooth Version: Bluetooth 5.0 or higher ensures faster pairing and better range.
  • Mic Support: A Bluetooth speaker with mic is essential if you take calls or use voice assistants.
  • Durability & Waterproofing: IP67 or IP68-rated models are ideal for poolside or outdoor use.
  • Bluetooth Speaker Price Ranges:
    • Budget: ₹1,000–₹5,000
    • Mid-range: ₹5,000–₹15,000
    • Premium: ₹15,000–₹40,000

Our Premium Collection of Bose Soundbars

Here’s a closer look at some of the most sought-after models we offer and service:

1. Bose Smart Soundbar 900

The Bose Smart Soundbar 900 redefines immersive audio with Dolby Atmos® support and exclusive Bose TrueSpace™ spatial processing. With built-in voice assistants and Wi-Fi® streaming, this soundbar is perfect for smart home integration.

Ideal For: High-end home theater setups that demand depth and clarity.

2. Bose Smart Soundbar 700

Stylish, powerful, and intelligently designed, the Smart Soundbar 700 is one of the most iconic Bose soundbar models. It offers customized audio calibration using ADAPTiQ® and supports seamless streaming via Bluetooth®, Wi-Fi®, or Apple AirPlay 2.

Ideal For: A modern household seeking powerful sound without complexity.

3. Bose TV Speaker

A compact yet impactful Bose soundbar, the TV Speaker enhances TV dialogues and background music with crystal-clear precision. Perfect for smaller rooms or minimalist spaces.

Ideal For: Users who prioritize dialogue clarity over deep surround sound.

4. Bose Soundbar 500

Balancing form and function, this soundbar includes Bose’s proprietary QuietPort technology for deeper bass without distortion. Its low-profile design makes it the choice for sleek setups.

Ideal For: Small to medium rooms where sound performance meets subtle design.

5. Bose Smart Ultra Soundbar

The epitome of luxury audio. With advanced voice pickup, ultra-rich bass, and cinematic acoustics, this soundbar is a flagship model in Bose’s lineup.

Ideal For: Audiophiles and entertainment enthusiasts demanding nothing but the best.

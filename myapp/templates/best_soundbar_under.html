Features to Consider When Buying a Soundbar Under 20000

Whether you’re setting up a new entertainment center or upgrading from built-in TV speakers, knowing what features to prioritize is crucial. Here’s a checklist to guide you:

Audio Channels (2.1, 3.1, 5.1)

  • 2.1: Two speakers + one subwoofer — great for small to medium rooms.
  • 3.1 and above: Additional center channel enhances dialogue clarity.
  • 5.1: Surround sound with rear speakers — ideal if you're looking for a more immersive experience.

Each of these formats is available in the best soundbar under 20000, so you can choose based on room size and audio expectations.

Woofer Presence

As previously noted, a soundbar with woofer gives you an edge in terms of audio realism. Wireless subwoofers also eliminate cable clutter, adding convenience to performance.

Connectivity Options

  • HDMI ARC: Ensures high-quality audio transmission from TV to soundbar.
  • Optical: A step below HDMI ARC but still excellent.
  • Bluetooth: Essential for music streaming and hands-free use.
  • AUX/USB: Useful for older devices or flash drive media.

Remote Control and App Support

Some models even offer smartphone app control, allowing you to adjust volume, bass, or treble from your mobile device.

Soundbar vs. Bluetooth Speaker: What Should You Choose?

A common user question at our service centre is: "Should I buy a Bluetooth speaker or a soundbar?" The answer depends on use case:

  • Bluetooth speakers are more portable, designed for casual music listening or outdoor use. The bluetooth speaker price varies widely depending on brand and features.
  • Soundbars, on the other hand, are designed specifically as soundbar for TV, offering more refined sound stages, higher wattage, and usually an external woofer.

If your priority is improving TV audio and immersive sound, the best soundbar under 20000 is your clear choice.

FAQs

Q1. Is a soundbar better than TV speakers?

Absolutely. Even an entry-level soundbar for TV can drastically improve audio clarity, bass response, and spatial effects compared to built-in TV speakers.

Q2. Can I get a soundbar with woofer under 20000?

Yes. Many brands offer soundbar with woofer configurations below ₹20,000, including wireless subwoofers for added flexibility.

Q3. Does Bluetooth affect sound quality?

Modern Bluetooth codecs (like AAC and aptX) ensure minimal quality loss. If you’re buying a best soundbar under 20000, make sure it supports updated Bluetooth versions.

Q4. What’s the average Bluetooth speaker price vs. soundbar?

Bluetooth speaker prices range from ₹1,500 to ₹15,000. Soundbars, especially soundbar for TV, start around ₹6,000 and can go up to ₹20,000+ depending on features.

Q5. Can I connect the soundbar to non-smart TVs?

Yes. With AUX, Optical, or HDMI ARC, you can easily connect even older TVs to the best soundbar under 20000.
